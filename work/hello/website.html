Bhagavad gita:THE HOLY BOOK OF TEACHINGS

The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred Hindu scripture that is considered one of the most important texts in the religion. It is a part of the larger epic, the Mahabharata, and is a dialogue between Lord Krishna and Arjuna on the battlefield before the start of the Kurukshetra War. The Gita emphasizes the importance of devotion to God, selfless service, and detachment from the fruits of one's actions. Its teachings on the nature of existence, the importance of dharma, or righteous action, and the concept of karma, or the law of cause and effect have been influential on all peoples.
